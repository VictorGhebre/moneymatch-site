MoneyMatch Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

Overview

MoneyMatch: Couples Finance helps couples discover their money personalities, understand financial compatibility, and have better conversations about money. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

When you use MoneyMatch, we may collect the following information:

  • Sign in with Apple: Your Apple ID name and email address (which may be a private relay address if you choose to hide your email). This is used solely for authentication.
  • Financial personality data: Your responses to the Money Archetype quiz, financial values, goals, and childhood money story. This is used to generate your archetype and compatibility insights.
  • Budget and expense data: Expenses you manually enter or sync via Apple FinanceKit. This data is used to power your shared budget and spending trends.
  • Couple data: Partner invite codes and shared compatibility information, synced between partners via Apple CloudKit.

Information We Don't Collect

  • No bank login credentials (FinanceKit uses Apple's secure on-device framework)
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers or ad tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking sent to third-party analytics services
  • No contact list or phone data
  • No browsing history

Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device: All app data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you delete the app, this local data is deleted.
  • iCloud (CloudKit): Couple-shared data (compatibility scores, shared budgets, Money Date history) is synced between partners using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

MoneyMatch uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription purchases and trials. RevenueCat receives anonymized transaction data from Apple to manage your subscription status. They do not receive your personal information. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple CloudKit: Syncs shared couple data between partners via iCloud. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple FinanceKit: Provides on-device access to bank account and transaction data. No financial data leaves your device or is sent to our servers. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

MoneyMatch offers a Premium subscription ($8.99/month or $59.99/year) with a 14-day free trial. All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your financial and personality data is shared only with your partner through the app's couple-linking feature, which requires explicit consent from both partners.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device (removes all local data)
  • Signing out of your account in app settings
  • Contacting us to request full account and data deletion

Children's Privacy

MoneyMatch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
